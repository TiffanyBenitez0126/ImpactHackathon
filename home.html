"Don't just throw it away. Throw it our way."

Waste management and collection has always been a major problem here in the Philippines. This was worsened by several lockdown protocols, as this led to a high influx in consumption of COVID-19 GEAR. Face Masks, Gloves, tissues, and such are not being properly disposed of and are often not segregated from solid wastes. The team presents its solution to enhance the management and collection of solid and hazardous wastes during several lockdown protocols through this website.
